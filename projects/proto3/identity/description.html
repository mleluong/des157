Identity Signs

Workshop Description

This workshop aims to highlight how different identities may be more or less salient in different people's everyday lives. You will be examining your personal identities to see how your daily experiences affect how you perceive yourself and others.

Instructions

  1. Answer the questions by clicking on the identity that you feels answers the question best for you.
  2. You may feel that more than one identity answers the question, but pick only one!
  3. After answering the question, you will have the option to reflect on why you chose the answer.
  4. Or, you can finish all the questions first, then reflect on all of your answers afterward!
  5. After reflecting on your answers, you can see what other people chose and their explanations.
  6. At the end of the workshop, you may publish your own answers for other users to see. If you're too shy, you can also save your responses as a PDF!
