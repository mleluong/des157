Audience Test

Audience Tasks

  1. Navigate across all pages of website.
  2. Navigate through all steps of workshop.
  3. Go back to the homepage after the workshop.

Questions for Tester

  1. Was it easy to navigate through the website?
  2. Did the flow of the workshop make sense?
  3. What are some features that you think can improve the website/workshop?
  4. How was the visual presentation of the website and workshop? In what ways could it improve?
  5. How could the workshop be more visually immersive?
  6. Would you be interested in more interactive workshops about social justice?
